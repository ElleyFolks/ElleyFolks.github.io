Over the past year, I’ve deepened my focus on machine learning, training models using TensorFlow and PyTorch to solve real-world problems. In my current internship, I’ve especially enjoyed collecting, cleaning, and analyzing data—building technical workflows and an appreciation for how raw data becomes meaningful insight. I’ve recently been exploring digital signal processing and its intersection with machine learning, which has expanded my interest in real-time systems and networks.

I’m proficient in several programming languages, including Python, Java, C++, HTML, and CSS, and I’ve also worked with the Unity engine to develop various game projects. I thrive on learning new tools and techniques, constantly evolving to keep pace with a fast-moving tech landscape.

I am curious, tenacious, and enthusiastic—a pursuer of excellence and knowledge. I hold myself to high standards and take pride in a disciplined, thoughtful work ethic grounded in prioritization and adaptability. I see every challenge as an opportunity for growth. Whether I succeed or fail, I learn—and I iterate.

When working in teams, I draw from the skills, knowledge, and perspectives of others. I value clear communication and am not afraid to ask questions, seek feedback, or offer support. Collaboration, to me, means leveraging the best of what each person brings to the table.

Outside of work and study, I love the outdoors. You can often find me hiking, camping, bouldering, or digging for crystals. I’m an avid reader (Wheel of Time and Dune are among my favorites) and enjoy gaming with friends during my downtime.
